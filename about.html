About

This website is a question bank for past NCEA exam questions and exam-like questions to help you revise for your external exams. To get the most out of this tool, try as many problems as you can. Mark yourself against the solution and self-mark by clicking the incorrect or correct button. If you weren't able to answer a question, try read the hint first, and if that doesn't help you can watch the video tutorial going over the question in more depth. Check the 'Stats' page to see how many questions you've answered and what topics you do well in or need to focus on.


This website is NOT affiliated with NCEA or NZQA. All questions taken from past NCEA exams are acknowledged in the details section of each question where NZQA is the copyright holder.
